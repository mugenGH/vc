# Asylum of Echoes - Game Complete

A psychological horror text adventure game featuring an atmospheric asylum setting with multiple endings based on your choices.

## How to Play

1. **Open the Game**: Simply open `index.html` in any modern web browser
2. **Start a New Game**: Click "Start New Game" to begin your journey
3. **Make Choices**: Select dialogue options to progress through the story
4. **Solve Puzzles**: Complete puzzles by entering codes or answering riddles
5. **Manage Inventory**: Collect items throughout the game (max 8 items)
6. **Build Relationships**: Your interactions with NPCs affect their trust in you
7. **Reach an Ending**: Your choices determine which of 5 endings you receive

## Game Features

- **5 Unique Endings**:
  - HOPE: Escape with both companions
  - SACRIFICE: Escape after the Troll's sacrifice
  - TRUTH: Discover the building's dark secrets
  - ACCEPTANCE: Become part of the asylum
  - ALONE: Escape by yourself

- **Dynamic Dialogue**: NPCs react based on your trust level
- **Puzzle System**: Solve codes and riddles to progress
- **Save System**: 3 manual save slots + auto-save
- **Audio Effects**: Atmospheric sound effects (can be toggled)
- **Progressive Atmosphere**: The sense of dread increases as you uncover clues

## Game Mechanics

### Trust System

- Each NPC has a trust value (-100 to +100)
- Your choices affect their trust
- Higher trust influences dialogue and ending conditions

### Clue System

- Collect clues by examining objects and solving puzzles
- Fear level increases as you gather information
- Total clues affect atmosphere intensity

### Inventory System

- Collect up to 8 items
- Items provide context and solutions to puzzles
- Hover over items to see descriptions

## Tips for Playing

1. **Talk to NPCs Often**: They provide hints and story context
2. **Examine Everything**: Take time to read descriptions
3. **Build Trust**: Being kind to companions has benefits
4. **Solve Puzzles**: Hidden items and progressed require solving puzzles
5. **Save Regularly**: Use the save system to explore different paths

## Controls

- **Click Buttons**: Navigate through choices and actions
- **Enter Text**: Solve puzzles by typing answers
- **Hint Buttons**: Get help solving puzzles
- **Audio Toggle**: Turn sound effects on/off

## Story Summary

You enter an abandoned psychiatric asylum searching for a missing person. You meet two inhabitants - Pip (a dwarf) and The Troll - who've been trapped inside far longer than seems possible. As you explore the building and uncover its secrets, you must decide whether to help them escape, sacrifice yourself for them, or accept the building itself.

The building has secrets. Subject 47. The Anaconda. And something that might be worse than any monster.

## Endings Guide

- **HOPE**: Requires befriending both NPCs and weakening the entity
- **SACRIFICE**: The Troll must hold back the darkness for you to escape
- **TRUTH**: You discover the building's true purpose
- **ACCEPTANCE**: You choose to stay and become a guardian
- **ALONE**: Both NPCs are dead
