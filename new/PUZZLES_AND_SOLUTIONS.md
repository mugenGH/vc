# Asylum of Echoes - Puzzle Solutions & Tips

## Game Puzzles

### PUZZLE 1: First Door Code (Office)

**Location**: Administrative Office - Locked desk drawer

**Clue**: The director's portrait lists a birth year

**Solution**: `1947`

**How to Find It**:

- Examine the portrait in the office carefully
- Look for numbers or dates mentioned in the description
- The portrait will reveal the director's birth year

**Reward**: Records Folder (Subject 47) - Important for understanding the story

---

### PUZZLE 2: Cabinet Code (Medical Ward)

**Location**: Medical Ward - Treatment Room Cabinet

**Clue**: Look at the treatment chart (FEAR, COMPLIANCE, ISOLATION, ACCEPTANCE, DISSOLUTION)

**Solution**: `739`

**How to Find It**:

- Examine the treatment chart on the wall
- Count the letters in each word that's circled
- The worn numbers on the keypad are: 7, 3, 9

**Reward**:

- Patient Journal
- Medicine Vials (Red & Blue)
- Strange Key (Basement)

---

### PUZZLE 3: Cell Scratches (Secured Ward)

**Location**: Secured Ward - Prison Cell Walls

**Clue**: The scratches read: "I AM HERE. I CANNOT LEAVE. IT CANNOT LEAVE. WE ARE THE SAME."

**Solution**: `CANNOT`

**How to Find It**:

- Read the scratches carefully
- Look for the most frequently repeated word
- Focus on what connects both the prisoner and the entity

**Reward**: Unlocks passage to Basement (Chapter 3)

## Optional Mechanics

### Relationship Building

- **Trust > +40**: NPCs offer better dialogue and support
- **Trust 0 to +40**: NPCs are neutral but helpful
- **Trust < 0**: NPCs become suspicious or hostile

### Item Usage Tips

1. **Handwritten Note**: Contains important context
2. **Patient Journal**: Reveals true nature of Subject 47
3. **Records Folder**: Essential for TRUTH ending
4. **Medicine Vials**: Environmental storytelling
5. **Strange Key**: Atmospheric element

### Clue Collection

Collect clues to unlock atmosphere progressions:

1. Examine portrait (office)
2. Search files (office)
3. Study treatment chart (medical)
4. Examine cabinet (medical)
5. Listen to walls (hallway)
6. Read scratches (cell)
7. Solve puzzles (various)

## Dialogue Trees

### Getting Better Dialogue

- **Early Game**: Talk to Pip and The Troll at the entrance
- **Mid Game**: Talk to Pip before solving major puzzles
- **Late Game**: Dialogue changes based on your sacrifice choices

### Key Conversation Points

1. **Entrance**: First meeting shapes future interactions
2. **Hallway**: The Troll hints at what haunts the building
3. **Office**: Pip warns you about the building's consciousness
4. **Medical**: The treatment chart is explained by atmosphere
5. **Cell**: NPCs react to the scratches
6. **Basement**: Critical choices that determine ending

## Ending Conditions

### HOPE Ending Requirements:

- ✓ Befriend both Pip and The Troll (trust > +20)
- ✓ Understand the building's nature (learned_truth)
- ✓ Weaken the entity (anaconda_weakening)
- ✓ Don't sacrifice The Troll
- ✓ Reach the exit and push through

### SACRIFICE Ending Requirements:

- ✓ The Troll dies protecting you
- ✓ Pip lives (trust > +30)
- ✓ Escape successfully

### TRUTH Ending Requirements:

- ✓ Discover Subject 47's identity
- ✓ Find the Records Folder
- ✓ At least one companion survives
- ✓ Pip trusts you (trust > +30)

### ACCEPTANCE Ending Requirements:

- ✓ See the white skeleton in the cell
- ✓ Understand what the building is
- ✓ Choose to stay behind
- ✓ Both NPCs trust you

### ALONE Ending Requirements:

- ✓ Both Pip and The Troll die
- ✓ Escape anyway

## Hidden Details & Lore

### Subject 47

- Not a person from outside
- Something the building "created" or "collected"
- The connection between the prisoner and entity
- Key to understanding the TRUTH

### The Anaconda

- The entity haunting the building
- Grows stronger from fear and trauma
- Can be weakened but not destroyed
- Remembers everyone who enters

### The Building Itself

- Acts as more than just a location
- Seems to have consciousness
- Remembers its inhabitants
- Chooses who leaves and who stays

## Troubleshooting

**Save File Missing?**

- Browser storage is cleared: Check privacy settings
- Incognito mode: Use normal browsing mode

**Audio Not Working?**

- Check browser permissions for audio
- Try toggling "Audio: On/Off" button

**Game Won't Progress?**

- Ensure you've talked to NPCs
- Check inventory for required items
- Try re-reading the scene description
